Lady Louise Alice Elizabeth Mary Mountbatten-Windsor, generally known as Lady Louise Windsor, is the elder child of Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex, and Sophie, Countess of Wessex, members of the British royal family. She is a granddaughter of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, and is 16th in the line of succession to the British throne as of September 2021.

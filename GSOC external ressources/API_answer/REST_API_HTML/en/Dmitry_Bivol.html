Dmitry Yuryevich Bivol is a Russian professional boxer who has held the WBA light-heavyweight title since 2017. As an amateur, he won a gold medal at the 2013 World Combat Games in the 81 kg weight category. As of August 2022, Bivol is ranked as the world's second best active boxer, pound for pound, by BoxRec; seventh by The Ring and the Boxing Writers Association of America, and eighth by the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board (TBRB) and ESPN. He is also ranked as the best light heavyweight by The Ring and BoxRec, and second by TBRB and ESPN.

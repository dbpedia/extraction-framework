Beetlejuice is a 1988 American fantasy horror comedy film directed by Tim Burton, produced by The Geffen Company, and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The plot revolves around a recently deceased couple who, as ghosts haunting their former home, contact Betelgeuse, an obnoxious and devious \"bio-exorcist\" from the Netherworld, to scare away the house's new inhabitants.

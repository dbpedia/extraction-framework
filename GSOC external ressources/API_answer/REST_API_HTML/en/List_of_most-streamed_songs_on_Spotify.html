The following list contains the top 100 songs with the most streams on the audio streaming platform Spotify. As of June 2022, only two songs have reached 3 billion streams, those two songs being Ed Sheeran's \"Shape of You\" and The Weeknd's \"Blinding Lights\", with \"Shape of You\" ranked in the top position. A total of 16 songs have reached 2 billion streams. As of August 2022, all of the songs on the list have reached 1.4 billion streams. Justin Bieber has the most songs in the top 100 with six entries. Post Malone has the most songs in the top 10 with two entries.

David Khari Webber Chappelle is an American stand-up comedian and actor. He has received numerous accolades, including five Emmy Awards and three Grammy Awards. In 2006, Chappelle was called the \"comic genius of America\" by Esquire and, in 2013, \"the best\" by a Billboard writer. In 2017, Rolling Stone ranked him No. 9 in their \"50 Best Stand Up Comics of All Time\". In 2019, Chappelle received the Mark Twain Prize for American Humor, presented by the Kennedy Center as America's highest comedy honor.

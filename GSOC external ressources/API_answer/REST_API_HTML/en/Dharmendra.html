Dharam Singh Deol, known mononymously as Dharmendra, is an Indian actor, producer and politician who is known for his work in Hindi films. Known as the \"He-Man\" of Bollywood, Dharmendra has worked in over 301 films in a career spanning six decades. In 1997, he received the Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award for his contribution to Hindi cinema.\nHe was a member of the 15th Lok Sabha of India, representing Bikaner constituency in Rajasthan from Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). In 2012, he was awarded India's third-highest civilian honour Padma Bhushan by the Government of India.

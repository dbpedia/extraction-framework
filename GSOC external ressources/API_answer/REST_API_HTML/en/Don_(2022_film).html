Don is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language coming-of-age film written and directed by Cibi Chakaravarthi in his directorial debut and produced by Allirajah Subaskaran and Sivakarthikeyan under their banners Lyca Productions and Sivakarthikeyan Productions. It stars Sivakarthikeyan in the lead role along with S. J. Suryah, Samuthirakani, Priyanka Arul Mohan and Soori. The film revolves around a reluctant engineering student repeatedly clashing with his college's discipline mentor, while still trying to discover his passion in life and prove his eligibility to his father.

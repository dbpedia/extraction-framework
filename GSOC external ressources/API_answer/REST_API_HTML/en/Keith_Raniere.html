Keith Allen Raniere is an American racketeer convicted for a pattern of offenses including human trafficking, sex offenses, and fraud. He co-founded NXIVM, a multi-level marketing company headquartered near Albany, New York. Media coverage and longtime observers of the group describe NXIVM as a \"cult\" due to the grandiosity of Raniere's claimed genius as well as the coercive control Raniere exerted over followers' lives.

HIT: The First Case is a 2020 Indian Telugu-language action-thriller film directed by debutant Sailesh Kolanu and produced by Prashanti Tipirneni. The film stars Vishwak Sen and Ruhani Sharma. The plot follows Vikram Rudraraju, a police officer of the Telangana state's Homicide Intervention Team (HIT) who is tasked to investigate the missing case of a young girl.

Javier Ángel Encinas Bardem is a Spanish actor. Known for his roles in blockbusters and foreign films, he has received various accolades, including an Academy Award, BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe Award, Cannes Film Festival Award for Best Actor, two Venice Film Festival Best Actor Awards, and six Goya Awards. He is the first Spanish actor to be nominated for an Academy Award (Best Actor for Before Night Falls, as well as the only Spanish actor to win one for No Country for Old Men.

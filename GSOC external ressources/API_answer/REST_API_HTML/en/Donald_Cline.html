Donald Lee Cline is a former American medical doctor of obstetrics & gynecology, a United States Air Force veteran, and a felon. It is believed that between 1974 and 1987, Cline conceived numerous children without disclosing himself as the sperm donor to his patients. In 2017, he was convicted on six obstruction of justice felonies connected to a fertility fraud investigation. As of May 11, 2022, Cline has been confirmed as the biological father of 94 doctor-conceived offspring.

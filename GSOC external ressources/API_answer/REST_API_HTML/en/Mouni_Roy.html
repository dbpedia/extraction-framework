Mouni Roy Nambiar is an Indian actress who primarily works in Hindi films and television. Roy became one of the highest-paid Hindi television actresses for starring as a shape-shifting serpent in the supernatural thriller Naagin (2015–2016) on Colors TV. She reprised her role in Naagin 2 (2016-2017).

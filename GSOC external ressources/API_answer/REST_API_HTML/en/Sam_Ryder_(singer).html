Sam Ryder is a British singer-songwriter. Ryder rose to prominence through TikTok, after posting music covers during the first lockdown of the COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020. He represented the United Kingdom at the Eurovision Song Contest 2022 with the song \"Space Man\" and finished first in the jury vote, and second overall.

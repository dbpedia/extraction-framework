Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi also known as (Khatron Ke Khiladi) is an Indian Hindi-language stunt based reality television series based on the American series Fear Factor. First launched as Fear Factor India on Sony TV it was sold to Colors TV and was relaunched as Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi on 21 July 2008. A Spin-off of the main-series Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi – Made in India was launched on 1st August 2020 where previous year contestants participated.

Indian 2 is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language vigilante action thriller film directed by S. Shankar and produced by Lyca Productions. A sequel to Indian (1996), it stars Kamal Haasan, Karthik, Kajal Aggarwal, Siddharth, Rakul Preet Singh, Priya Bhavani Shankar, Bobby Simha, Guru Somasundaram and Samuthirakani. The film has music composed by Anirudh Ravichander, with cinematography by Ravi Varman and Rathnavelu and editing by A. Sreekar Prasad.

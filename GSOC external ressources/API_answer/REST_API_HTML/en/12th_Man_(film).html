12th Man is a 2022 Indian Malayalam-language mystery thriller film directed by Jeethu Joseph and produced by Antony Perumbavoor through Aashirvad Cinemas, with a screenplay by K. R. Krishna Kumar and based on a story by Sunir Khetarpal. The film stars Mohanlal, with Saiju Kurup, Leona Lishoy, Chandhunath, Unni Mukundan, Anu Sithara, Anusree, Anu Mohan, Rahul Madhav, Aditi Ravi, Priyanka Nair and Sshivada. This film is loosely based on the core idea of 2016 Italian film Perfect Strangers.

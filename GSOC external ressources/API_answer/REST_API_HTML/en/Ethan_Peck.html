Ethan Gregory Peck is an American actor. He is the grandson of actor Gregory Peck and Greta Kukkonen, Peck's first wife. In 2019, he played a young Spock in Star Trek: Discovery (2019), a role he has reprised for the television series Star Trek: Strange New Worlds (2022–present).

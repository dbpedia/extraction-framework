The SCP Foundation is a fictional secret organization documented by the collaborative writing wiki project of the same name, with the abbreviation standing for \"Special Containment Procedures\". Within the website's shared universe, the Foundation is responsible for capturing and containing various paranormal, supernatural, and other mysterious phenomena unexplained by mainstream science, while also keeping their existence hidden from the rest of human society. The real-world website is community-based, originating in 4chan, and includes elements of many genres such as horror, science fiction, and urban fantasy.

Kartik Aaryan Tiwari, is an Indian actor who works in Hindi films. After pursuing a degree in engineering, he made his acting debut in 2011 with Luv Ranjan's buddy film Pyaar Ka Punchnama. He went on to act in Akaash Vani (2013) and Kaanchi: The Unbreakable (2014).
